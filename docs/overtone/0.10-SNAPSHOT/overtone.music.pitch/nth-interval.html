nth-interval

Return the count of semitones for the nth degree from the start of the diatonic scale in the specific mode (or ionian/major by default). i.e. the ionian/major scale has an interval sequence of 2 2 1 2 2 2 1 therefore the 4th degree is (+ 2 2 1 2) semitones from the start of the scale.

      (defn nth-interval
  "Return the count of semitones for the nth degree from the start of
  the diatonic scale in the specific mode (or ionian/major by
  default).

  i.e. the ionian/major scale has an interval sequence of 2 2 1 2 2 2
       1 therefore the 4th degree is (+ 2 2 1 2) semitones from the
       start of the scale."
  ([n] (nth-interval :diatonic n))
  ([scale n]
     (reduce + (take n (cycle (scale SCALE))))))
