pluck

[in 0.0, trig 1.0, maxdelaytime 0.2, delaytime 0.2, decaytime 1.0, coef 0.5] in - An excitation signal. trig - Upon a negative to positive transition, the excitation signal will be fed into the delay line. maxdelaytime - The max delay time in seconds (initializes the internal delay buffer). delaytime - Delay time in seconds. decaytime - Time for the echoes to decay by 60 decibels. Negative times emphasize odd partials. coef - The coef of the internal OnePole filter. Values should be between -1 and +1 (larger values will be unstable... so be careful!). Implements the Karplus-Strong style of synthesis, where a delay line (normally starting with noise) is filtered and fed back on itself so that over time it becomes periodic.
